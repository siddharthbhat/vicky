About

This web application is a group project developed by students of Dublin City University (DCU).

As part of our assignment, we aimed to build a comprehensive data dashboard tailored for the DCU campus environment.

Our goal is to offer an intuitive platform that enables users to access historical data, visualize trends, and make informed decisions based on the insights provided.

Thank you for visiting our website!
